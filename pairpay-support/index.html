Common Questions

  • How do I add an expense?
    Tap “Add Expense,” choose a category, enter the amount, who paid, and how it should be split. Save it and you're done.
  • How do I settle up?
    Go to the summary screen and tap “Settle Up” to log a payment and reset your shared balance.
  • Can I edit or delete expenses?
    Yes! Tap any expense in the list to edit or delete it.
