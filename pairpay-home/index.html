PairPay

PairPay is the easiest way for couples to track and split shared expenses — especially when you don’t always split things 50/50.

What you can do with PairPay:

  • Track who paid and who owes
  • Split expenses by percentages or fractions
  • See monthly summaries and category breakdowns
  • Settle balances anytime
  • Get AI-powered insights on spending (coming soon)

Built for couples. No spreadsheets. No stress.
